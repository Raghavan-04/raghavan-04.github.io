The Origin

The journey began. A curiosity for the world and the sounds that fill it was sparked.

2008

Love at First Sight

Witnessed the raw power of F2 and F3 racing in Chettipalayam. The roar of the engines and the blistering speed sparked a lifelong obsession with cars and motorsport.

2014

Visual Discovery

Picked up my first Camera. Learned that photography isn't just about capturing light, but about freezing emotion in time.

2019

High Fidelity

It started with the hunt for a Bose Acoustimass 10 Series V. When the 5.1 system went out of stock right before purchase, it became a catalyst. I dove deep into the science of sound and acoustics, ultimately choosing to engineer and build my own custom speakers from scratch with help of dad.

2020

The Analog Shift

Stepped back from digital to embrace 35mm film. The deliberate, unforgiving process of analog taught patience and intention.

2024

Sonic Spaces

Constructed my first acoustically treated listening environment. Realized that the room itself is the most important instrument in playback.

2026
